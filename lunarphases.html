Lunar Phases

Poster Design · Visual Identity · Typography · 2025

This project focuses on organizing a lot of information in a way that still feels clear and easy to follow. I used a circular layout to reflect the movement of the moon, so the structure of the page connects directly to the idea of a continuous cycle.

I kept the colors simple with a dark background and bright yellow so the phases stand out right away. The moon shapes are simplified and repeated throughout the poster to keep everything consistent and easy to read.

I also paid close attention to spacing, hierarchy, and balance so the text would not feel too heavy. I wanted the poster to guide the viewer through the information step by step while still feeling visually engaging.
