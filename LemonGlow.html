LemonGlow
Brand Guidelines

Branding · Packaging · Illustration · 2025

LemonGlow is a conceptual beauty brand inspired by bright citrus + that clean “fresh out the shower” feeling. I wanted it to feel playful but still polished—like something you’d actually see in a store.

For this project, I designed the logo system, color palette, packaging layouts, and the final product mockup photos. I treated it like a real brand kit, where everything has to match and feel consistent.

I built the palette around citrus tones—bright yellow for the “lemon” moment, and warm oranges/browns to keep it feeling rich instead of neon. I wanted the colors to feel energetic but still clean and wearable, like a real product line.
