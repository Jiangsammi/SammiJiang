LIMESALT

Rebrand · Menu Design · Brand Applications · 2025

LIMESALT is a rebrand of the brand’s original menu design. For this project, I focused on making the menu easier for customers to read and move through while still keeping the brand feeling fresh, fun, and recognizable.

A big part of this redesign was improving hierarchy. I reorganized the menu so the most important information stands out first, and each section feels more structured and easier to scan.

Typography

For LIMESALT, I wanted the typography to feel fresh, bold, and easy to read. I used the type system to build clearer hierarchy across the menu, helping section titles, item names, and descriptions feel more organized while still matching the brand’s playful personality.
