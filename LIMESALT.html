LIMESALT

Rebrand · Menu Design · Brand Applications · 2025

LIMESALT is a rebrand of the original menu design for the brand. For this project, I rebranded the original LIMESALT menu with a focus on readability and layout. I wanted to make the information easier for customers to look through while still keeping the brand feeling fresh, fun, and recognizable.

A big part of this redesign was improving hierarchy. I reorganized the menu so the most important information stands out first, and so each section feels more structured and easier to scan. This helps the customer move through the menu faster without feeling overwhelmed.

Typography

For LIMESALT, I wanted the typography to feel fresh, bold, and easy to read. I used the type system to build clearer hierarchy across the menu, helping section titles, item names, and descriptions feel more organized while still matching the brand’s playful personality.
