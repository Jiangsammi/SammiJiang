Study Sprout is an app I designed to help students better manage and navigate their schoolwork. It connects to platforms like Canvas so assignments, due dates, and grades can be viewed in one organized place.

The app includes AI tools for quick summaries, translations, and email drafting, along with focus features such as reminders, task lists, and timers to support productivity.

To make studying feel less boring, the app includes a small character feature that students can take care of. Students choose a character and earn coins by completing assignments before deadlines, which can be used to care for their character.

Reflection

I created Study Sprout in an AI design course to explore how technology could make studying feel less stressful. I wanted the app to feel supportive, not overwhelming. This project helped me think more deeply about motivation, balance, and how design can make everyday tasks feel a little more encouraging.
