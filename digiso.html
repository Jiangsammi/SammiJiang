DIGISO

Branding · Print Design · Marketing · 2025

DIGISO is a project based on the work I created while working as a Marketing Assistant at a Korean beauty retail store. I designed promotional materials such as hiring posters, discount tags, business cards, and Black Friday graphics. I focus on creating approachable advertisements that fit the store’s aesthetic while keeping the designs clear, simple, and easy for customers to understand.
