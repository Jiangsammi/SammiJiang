Guerrilla Girls

Print Files · Mobile App · Map · 2025

Home is a visual storytelling project that explores family, memory, and care through quiet cultural moments and traditional imagery. Inspired by East Asian aesthetics, the book uses soft colors, textured backgrounds, and historical illustrations to create a calm and reflective atmosphere. The design focuses on simplicity, balance, and space to highlight everyday rituals like tea drinking, sharing food, and conversation. Through these elements, the project presents home not as a fixed place, but as a feeling shaped by culture, memory, and human connection. This project reflects on family, memory, care, and the emotional nourishment that home provides through visual storytelling and curated text, inviting the reader to see home as something layered, tender, and always shifting—held together, like a bento, by quiet acts of care.
