Little Cupcake
Brand Guidelines

Branding · Packaging · Illustration · 2025

Little Cupcake Bakery is a brand and website project centered around warmth, nostalgia, and small everyday joy. I wanted the identity to feel sweet without being childish — playful but still refined. The design focuses on soft tones, handmade details, and clear structure to create a welcoming digital experience that feels like stepping into a cozy neighborhood bakery.

Logo Variations

The logo combines soft curves and hand-drawn lettering to reflect the bakery’s homemade feel. I designed multiple variations so the identity can adapt across packaging, social media, and signage while still feeling consistent. The goal was to keep it charming but balanced — expressive without losing clarity.

The color palette is inspired by baked goods — warm reds, chocolate browns, and soft blush pinks. I chose tones that feel comforting and familiar, while still giving the brand enough contrast to feel polished. The softer pinks add sweetness, and the deeper shades ground the identity.

Typography

The typography blends expressive, slightly playful lettering with structured serif fonts. I wanted the type to feel warm and inviting, but still clean and readable across digital and print applications. The combination keeps the brand approachable while maintaining a professional finish.
