MATCHA GIRL

Typography · Poster Design · Digital Collage · 2025

Matcha Girl explores how matcha has become more than just a drink. It has turned into a lifestyle image connected to social media, beauty, routine, and identity. This project uses typography, posters, and digital collage to show that cultural shift in a playful and expressive way.

Logo Variations

Typography

I used Destomed for the more expressive text, Apple Chancery for the decorative type, and Arial for the body text. I also used a bold sans-serif font in the poster to make the main phrase stand out more.

Destomed — 117 pt
Apple Chancery — 72 pt
Arial — body text

Poster Design

Typography Exercise

This was a typography challenge from my Systems for Branding class. I used the same Matcha text and tried three different layouts, changing the spacing, weight, and composition to see how the hierarchy could shift.
